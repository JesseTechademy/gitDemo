Coyotes

Here is some information about the coyote

The coyote is about 60cm off the ground at its shoulder, and is approximately 1 to 1,3 meters in length, including the tail which can be 30 - 40 cm.
The coyote weights anywhere between 9 and 23 kilogrammes
Coyotes can reach a top speed of around 64 kilometers per hour, and mainly hunt during the night.
Most commonly found in North America in the mountains or plains habitat.
